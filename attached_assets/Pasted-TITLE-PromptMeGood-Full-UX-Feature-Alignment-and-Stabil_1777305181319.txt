TITLE: PromptMeGood Full UX, Feature Alignment, and Stability Audit (Implementation Plan)

---

## 🎯 CORE OBJECTIVE

Refine and elevate the existing PromptMeGood application so that:

* All current features remain intact (DO NOT REMOVE ANY FEATURES)
* The user experience is intuitive for beginners
* All UI/UX messaging reflects current AI-powered capabilities
* Friction is minimized across desktop and mobile
* The product feels trustworthy, polished, and worth paying for

---

## ⚠️ NON-NEGOTIABLE RULES

* Do NOT remove any features
* Do NOT break existing functionality
* Do NOT expose API keys
* Do NOT alter backend logic unless explicitly required for stability
* Only refine, reposition, clarify, and enhance

---

## 🧠 TARGET USER

Primary user:

> The everyday person who wants AI to actually understand them

Implications:

* They do NOT understand prompts
* They do NOT care about technical settings
* They want clarity, speed, and results
* They need guidance without feeling overwhelmed
