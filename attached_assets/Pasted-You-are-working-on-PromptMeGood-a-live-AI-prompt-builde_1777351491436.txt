WHAT THIS PRODUCT IS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

PromptMeGood helps everyday people get better results 
from AI. User types a goal, clicks Fix My Prompt, gets 
a structured prompt, then runs it with AI in-app or 
copies it to ChatGPT, Claude, or Perplexity. It also 
has DALL-E 3 image generation in Create An Image mode.

Primary user: everyday people who use AI and get 
frustrated because AI doesn't understand what they mean.
