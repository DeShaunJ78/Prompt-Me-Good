FAILURE 4: Pointer/click interception still exists

Observed:
During mobile regression, clicking the visible `Run With AI` button timed out because:
- `#result-panel` intercepted pointer events, and/or
- top beta banner link intercepted pointer events after scroll.

This also previously happened with Photography Suite controls.

Fix:
Audit sticky/overlay/z-index/pointer-events layers:
- `#pmg-t42-beta-banner`
- `.topbar`
- `#result-panel`
- `.pmg-result-sticky`
- closed tour overlay/backdrop
- hidden replay/dropdown/menu layers
- any fixed/sticky element with pointer-events enabled when not visually covering the target.

Rules:
- Hidden/closed overlays and dropdowns must use `pointer-events: none`.
- Sticky banners/headers must not intercept clicks on elements they are not visually covering.
- If scroll positioning places a target under a fixed banner/header, adjust scroll offset or use `scroll-margin-top`.
- Active buttons inside result panel should be on top of panel background and clickable.
- Do not set pointer-events:none on the whole result panel if it disables child buttons. If needed, set pointer-events on decorative overlay only.

Click acceptance:
At 390×844:
- After Fix My Prompt, clicking the visible top `Run With AI` button works with a normal browser click.
- No `#result-panel` or beta banner intercepts that click.
- Photography Suite controls such as Surprise Me / photo controls can be clicked normally after scrolling.
- No invisible tour/menu overlay intercepts clicks after tours are closed.
