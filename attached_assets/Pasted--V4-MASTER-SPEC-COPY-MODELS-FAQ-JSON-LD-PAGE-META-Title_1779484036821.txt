─── FAQ SECTION ─────────────────────────────────────────────────
H2: Frequently asked questions

Q1: When is PromptPerfect shutting down?
A1: PromptPerfect stops accepting new signups in June 2026 
    and goes fully offline on September 1, 2026. User data 
    is permanently deleted on October 1, 2026. Export your 
    prompts from Settings → Export Data before that date.

Q2: Can I import my PromptPerfect prompts into PromptMeGood?
A2: Yes. Export your prompt library from PromptPerfect as a 
    JSON file, then use the Import button in your PromptMeGood 
    Vault to bring everything across. Your prompts land 
    immediately — no manual copy-pasting required.

Q3: Is PromptMeGood free?
A3: Yes. PromptMeGood has a free plan with no credit card 
    required. New accounts also include a 7-day trial with 
    access to every feature so you can evaluate it fully 
    before deciding on a paid plan.

Q4: Does PromptMeGood work with ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini?
A4: Yes. PromptMeGood builds and optimizes prompts for 
    ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and Perplexity. Prompts are 
    plain text — you paste them wherever you work.

Q5: What does PromptMeGood do that PromptPerfect didn't?
A5: PromptMeGood adds prompt building from scratch, a 
    personality and voice system, image prompt generation, 
    image reverse-engineering, in-app prompt execution, 
    and a full Vault for organizing your library — while 
    keeping the core prompt optimization that PromptPerfect 
    was known for.

Q6: Is PromptMeGood going to shut down too?
A6: PromptMeGood is independently built and operated — 
    it is not part of a larger acquisition or corporate 
    product suite. There are no plans to shut it down.
