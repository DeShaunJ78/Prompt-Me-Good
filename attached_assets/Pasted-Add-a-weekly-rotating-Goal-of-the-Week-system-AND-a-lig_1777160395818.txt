========================
PART 1 — WEEKLY GOAL ROTATION
========================

GOAL:
Automatically update “Goal of the Week” every 7 days.

IMPLEMENT:

1. Create goal list:

const weeklyGoals = [
  "Make your first $100 online using AI",
  "Create a viral TikTok idea in your niche",
  "Write a cold email that actually gets replies",
  "Fix a business problem that’s costing you money",
  "Build a simple product you can sell this week",
  "Improve your resume for a better job opportunity",
  "Plan a side hustle you can start this month",
  "Write a high-converting product description",
  "Solve a real-life problem you’ve been avoiding",
  "Learn a new skill faster using AI"
];

2. Generate week index:

const weekIndex = Math.floor(Date.now() / (1000 * 60 * 60 * 24 * 7));
const goalIndex = weekIndex % weeklyGoals.length;

3. Display:

weeklyGoals[goalIndex]

4. Make clickable:
- Clicking auto-fills “Your Goal” field
