2. Clarifying Questions UX Improvement
The Problem: When the Auto-Boost feature asks clarifying questions, users are confused about how to respond because the input fields just say “Your answer (optional)”. They don’t know if they should write a full sentence, a single word, or what format is expected.

The Fix:
Improve the placeholder text and add micro-copy to guide the user.

	•	Change the placeholder text from “Your answer (optional)” to something more instructive, like “e.g., ‘Dark and moody’ or ‘Bright and sunny’”.
	•	Add a small helper text below the input fields: “Short answers are fine. You can also skip this step.”
	•	Ensure the “Got it, now boost” and “Skip, boost anyway” buttons are visually distinct, with the primary action being more prominent
