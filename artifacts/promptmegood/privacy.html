Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026.

What stays on your device. PromptMeGood is a single-page tool that runs primarily in your browser. Your prompts, history, templates, settings, and preferences are stored locally on your device using your browser's localStorage. We do not maintain user accounts and we do not have a copy of any of this on our servers.

What gets sent to AI providers. When you use an AI-powered feature — Generate Prompt, Run With AI, Upload File or Image, or Generate Image — the text you typed and any file or image you uploaded is sent securely to our AI service providers (such as OpenAI) so they can process your request and return a response. We do not store this content on our servers beyond what is required to complete the request.

What we never do. We do not sell your data. We do not share your prompts, files, or images with advertisers. We do not require you to create an account to use the app.

Anonymous usage analytics. We use Microsoft Clarity to understand anonymous, aggregated usage patterns (such as which buttons are clicked) so we can improve the experience. Clarity does not capture the text of your prompts.

You're in control. You can export, import, or clear your saved data at any time from the Prompt History section. Clearing your browser storage will remove everything PromptMeGood has saved on your device.

Questions? Email support@promptmegood.com.
