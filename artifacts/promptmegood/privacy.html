Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 28, 2026.

What stays on your device. PromptMeGood is a single-page tool that runs primarily in your browser. Your prompt history, templates, favorites, tags, notes, theme preference, Photography Suite picks, and other settings are stored locally on your device using your browser's localStorage. We do not maintain user accounts and we do not keep a permanent copy of your saved prompts on our servers.

What gets sent to AI providers. When you use an AI-powered feature — including Fix My Prompt, Run With AI, Help Me Start, Need Ideas, Photography Suite → Send To Image Generator, file analysis, or image generation (DALL·E 3) — your submitted text, photo-suite selections, and any uploaded content are sent securely over HTTPS to our AI service providers (such as OpenAI) to process your request. Files, prompts, photo briefs, and generated images are not permanently stored on our servers beyond what is required to fulfill your request and return the result to you. AI providers may retain inputs and outputs in accordance with their own privacy policies.

What we never do. We do not sell your data. We do not share your prompts, files, photo briefs, or images with advertisers. We do not require you to create an account to use the app. We do not train AI models on your data.

Pseudonymous usage analytics & session insights. We use Microsoft Clarity to understand de-identified, aggregated usage patterns so we can improve the experience. Clarity assigns a session identifier rather than collecting your name or contact details, but because session replay and device/IP-derived metadata are involved, this data is best described as pseudonymous rather than fully anonymous. It includes:

  • Click and scroll behavior — which buttons are tapped and how far people scroll on each page (heatmaps).
  • Anonymized session recordings — a replay of mouse movement, taps, and page navigation. Clarity automatically masks form fields and text inputs, so the contents of your goal field, generated prompts, and any text you type are not captured.
  • Frustration signals — aggregate counts of rage clicks, dead clicks, and quick page exits, used to find broken or confusing UI.
  • Approximate location, device, browser, and IP-derived metadata — collected by Clarity for analytics. We do not use IP addresses to personally identify you.

You can opt out of Microsoft Clarity collection across the web at privacy.microsoft.com, or by enabling Do Not Track / a tracker-blocking extension in your browser.

Cookies & local storage. We use a small number of first-party cookies and localStorage keys to remember your theme accent, light/dark preference, vault data, and analytics session. We do not use third-party advertising cookies.

You're in control. You can export, import, or clear your saved data at any time from the Prompt Vault section. Clearing your browser storage will remove everything PromptMeGood has saved on your device.

Your privacy rights (GDPR / UK GDPR / CCPA). Because PromptMeGood does not maintain user accounts, the only personal-ish data we typically hold about you is the pseudonymous Microsoft Clarity session metadata described above. If you are in the EU, UK, or California (or another jurisdiction with similar laws), you have the right to: (a) request access to any data we hold about you, (b) request correction or deletion, (c) object to or restrict processing, (d) data portability where applicable, and (e) lodge a complaint with your local data-protection authority. Email support@promptmegood.com with your request and we'll respond within a reasonable timeframe (typically 30 days). To exercise the "Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information" right under the CCPA: we already do not sell or share your personal information.

Data retention. Locally stored data (your vault, history, picks, settings) stays on your device until you clear it. AI provider request logs are subject to those providers' retention policies (typically up to 30 days). Microsoft Clarity session data is retained per Clarity's defaults (typically up to 13 months).

International transfers. Our AI providers and analytics vendor are based in the United States. By using PromptMeGood you understand that your inputs may be processed in the US under appropriate safeguards (such as Standard Contractual Clauses where required).

Children. PromptMeGood is intended for users 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will remove it.

Changes to this policy. We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision. Material changes will be highlighted in the app.

Questions or data requests? Email support@promptmegood.com.
