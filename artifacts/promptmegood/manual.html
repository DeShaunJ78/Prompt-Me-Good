🚀 1. Getting started

No signup. No download. The whole workstation runs in your browser, and your work stays on your device until you choose to send something to an AI provider.

Open the app for the first time

  1. Visit promptmegood.com and tap Open The App →.
  2. The workstation loads at /app. The first time you visit, the Text Prompts panel is active by default.
  3. Pick your theme using the moon/sun toggle in the top bar. We mirror your OS preference by default.

Open the app as a returning user

  1. Visit promptmegood.com again — if you've used the app before, you're redirected straight to /app.
  2. You'll see a Welcome Back Command Center with Continue Last Prompt, Start A New Prompt, and Open Workspace.
Want to bypass the redirect? Visit /?stay=1 to view the marketing landing page even if you're a returning user. Want to deep-link to a specific panel? Use /app?panel=photography or /app?panel=video.

🪟 2. The workstation layout

PromptMeGood uses a clean three-panel chassis. One topbar, one tab strip, one body that swaps between panels.

Top bar

App brand on the left, Vault and Settings buttons in the middle, and the Upgrade button on the right. The topbar is always visible.

Module tabs

Three pill tabs under the topbar: 📝 Text Prompts, 📸 Photography, 🎬 Video. Tap a tab to switch panels. The active tab is highlighted with mint.

Two-column body (desktop)

Each panel uses a 1fr 1fr grid. Left = builder (goal, picks, generate). Right = output (refined prompt + result). On mobile (≤768px) the columns stack — builder on top, output below.

Pull-to-refresh (mobile)

Swipe down from the top of the workstation to refresh — same gesture as native apps. Works on iOS Safari and Chrome on Android.

✍️ 3. Text Prompts panel

The default panel. Type plain English, get a structured, model-ready prompt for ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, Gemini — anything that takes text.

The goal box

The big textarea labeled What do you want AI to help you with? Drop one honest sentence in. The strength badge above turns green once you've added enough context, but you can generate at any strength.

Tuning controls

  • Tone — Friendly, Professional, Persuasive, Analytical, Playful, Authoritative, etc.
  • Length — Short, Medium, Long.
  • Format — Plain prose, Bullet list, Numbered steps, Table, Email, Outline, JSON.
  • Reading level — From "explain like I'm 12" to "domain expert".
  • Language — Output language (English, Spanish, French, German, Japanese, etc.).
  • Category — Optional hint (Marketing, Code, Study, Business, Creative…) that nudges the rewrite toward conventions of that field.

Generate Prompt

The big mint button. Your structured prompt — with role, context, constraints, tone, format, and next steps — appears in the right panel within a couple of seconds.

Refine in place

  • More Detailed — adds specifics, examples, and edge cases.
  • More Bold & Direct — strips hedging language.
  • Beginner Friendly — simplifies jargon, adds analogies.
  • Undo Last Change — steps back one refinement.

Run With AI

The ▶ Run With AI button runs your optimized prompt against GPT-4o right inside the app. The answer streams into the response panel below — no need to copy-paste.

File analyze

The 📎 paperclip icon attaches a PDF or image (≤10 MB). The AI reads the file before answering — perfect for "summarize this PDF" or "what's in this screenshot?"

📸 4. Photography panel

Build pro-level AI image prompts and generate them with DALL·E 3 / gpt-image-1.

The image goal box

Same idea as the text panel — describe the image you want in plain English. Then dial in the look using the Photo Suite below.

Photo Suite — six pill groups

Style

Photoreal, Editorial, Cinematic, Anime, Oil Painting, Watercolor, Sketch, 3D Render, Vector, Pixel Art, and more.

Camera & Lens (sub-grouped)

Focal Length (24mm wide → 200mm telephoto), Camera Body (Sony A7R, Canon R5, Hasselblad…), Aperture (f/1.4 dreamy → f/16 sharp), Shutter Speed, ISO, and Film Stock (Full Color, B&W, VHS, 16mm Film, 35mm Film, Digital Clean).

Camera Angle

Eye Level · Low Angle · High Angle · Hero Angle · Over-the-Shoulder · Profile Side · Top-Down Flat Lay · Bird's-Eye · Worm's-Eye · Dutch Angle.

Lighting & Mood

Golden Hour · Blue Hour · Midday · Twilight · Front Lit · Side Lit · Top Lit · Studio · Neon · Dramatic Backlight · Soft Window Light, etc.

Composition

Center Weighted, Headroom, Rule of Thirds, Extreme Close-Up, Medium Shot, Extreme Wide, Selective Focus, plus the classic angle picks.

Color Palette

Warm, Cool, Pastel, Monochrome, High Contrast, Earthy, Vibrant, Muted, etc.

On mobile, all six groups start collapsed — tap a header to expand. On desktop they're expanded by default. Each group also has a Quick Pick tinted card up top with one-click presets.

Build Image Prompt → Generate Image

Build Image Prompt composes your goal + picks into a refined prompt in the right panel. Generate Image sends it to OpenAI. The image lands in the result surface with Save, 🧬 DNA Card, ↗ Share, and Regenerate buttons.

🖼️ 5. Image Workshop

Inside the Photography panel. An AI photo lab for upscaling, restoring, restyling, and remixing existing images. Powered by OpenAI gpt-image-1.

Upload

  1. Drag a JPG, PNG, or WEBP onto the dropzone, or click to pick. Max 10 MB.
  2. The image preview appears in the dropzone.

Pick enhancement chips (stack as many as you want)

  • Upscale — sharper, higher-res output.
  • Color Pop — saturated, vivid color grade.
  • Cinematic Grade — moody film LUT.
  • Remove Background — clean cutout.
  • Restore Old Photo — fix scratches, fade, color shift.
  • Day↔Night — flips the time of day.
  • Vector Style — flat, logo-friendly look.
  • Anime · Oil Painting · Watercolor · B&W Film · 35mm Grain — style transfers.
  • Studio Lighting · Clean BG · HDR — production polish.

Optional note

A free-form text field for one-off requests ("make the sky more dramatic", "add bokeh to the background"). The model honors notes alongside the chips.

Enhance Photo

Hits POST /api/image-edit. Counts against your daily image cap (free tier 1/day, Founding 15/day, Pro 30/day).

Save as PNG / Save as SVG

Save as PNG downloads the result raw. Save as SVG wraps the result inside an SVG <image> element — pair it with the Vector Style chip for flat, logo-friendly output that stays scalable.

Composition is preserved. Every enhancement runs under a "preserve subject and composition" guardrail, so chips don't accidentally redraw the scene.

🔍 6. Reverse Engine — image-to-prompt

Below the image-goal box in the Photography panel. Upload any reference image and get back a usable AI image prompt plus matching Photo Suite picks.

How it works

  1. Tap 📸 Reverse Engineer an Image and pick a JPG, PNG, or WEBP (≤10 MB). Or drag a file straight onto the button — it doubles as a dropzone.
  2. The image is sent to GPT-4o vision via POST /api/vision-analyze.
  3. The image goal box is pre-filled with a usable AI prompt.
  4. Photo Suite pills are programmatically pressed to match the detected style, camera, lighting, composition, and palette — best-effort, silently no-ops on misses.
  5. Tweak any pill or text and hit Generate Image to render a variation.

Counts against your daily analyze cap (free tier 1/day, Founding 10/day, Pro 20/day).

🧬 7. Prompt DNA Card

After you generate an image, two extra buttons appear: 🧬 DNA Card and ↗ Share. They build a 1080×1350 PNG that pairs the image with the exact prompt that made it.

What's on the card

  • Top 1080×1080 — your generated image.
  • Bottom strip — the refined prompt text plus the PromptMeGood brand stamp.

How it works

  1. Pure-frontend rendering — composes a canvas locally, no upload to our servers.
  2. 🧬 DNA Card downloads the PNG.
  3. ↗ Share uses the Web Share API where available (mobile Safari, Chrome). Hidden on browsers that don't support it.
Cross-origin fallback. If the image is tainted (CORS-blocked), the card falls back to a brand-only design with a notice — your prompt is still readable.

Build Video Prompt composes your goal + picks. Generate Video hits POST /api/video. Free and trial tiers see an upgrade card linking to the Founding Member waitlist; Founding gets 5/day, Pro gets 10/day.

🎞️ 9. Storyboard generator

Inside the Video panel. Turn a story concept into a 5-shot cinematic storyboard with rendered images for the first two panels.

How it works

  1. Tap 🎞️ Generate Storyboard. A modal opens.
  2. Type a concept ("A barista falls in love with a regular over a year of seasons").
  3. Submit. POST /api/storyboard returns 5 shot descriptions.
  4. The first two panels render as images via parallel /api/image calls.
  5. Tap 🎬 Send to Video to switch to the Video panel and pre-fill the goal box with the full shot list ("Shot 1: … → Shot 2: …").

🗂 11. Vault & Templates

Save anything you generate. Local-first by default — your vault lives in your browser's localStorage. Optional cloud sync for signed-in members.

Save to Vault

The 💾 Save To Vault button appears next to every generated prompt. Add tags, a custom title, or a note before saving.

Browse

Open the Vault from the topbar. Search by keyword, filter by tag or date, pin favorites.

Compare

Open two saved prompts side-by-side to see exactly what changed.

Export / Import

Export the entire vault as a JSON file for backup. Import on another device or browser to restore.

Templates

28+ starter templates — emails, blog posts, code reviews, study guides, image briefs. Tap to load into the goal box.

⚙️ 12. Expert Command Center

A drawer with five tabs for advanced prompt work. Free during the open beta, paid feature after BETA_END.

  • Diagnose — runs your prompt through a quality checker (clarity, specificity, role, output format, edge cases).
  • Engineer — applies advanced techniques: chain-of-thought, ReAct, few-shot examples, output schemas.
  • Tune — sliders for temperature, verbosity, formality, and confidence.
  • Variations — generates 3–5 variants of the same prompt for A/B testing.
  • Save — version-controls your prompt with named snapshots.

🛠️ 18. Troubleshooting

Image won't generate

  1. Confirm you're on the Photography panel and your image goal isn't empty.
  2. Check you haven't hit the daily image cap (free tier = 1/day).
  3. Simplify the brief — extreme combinations of pills can occasionally fail.

Image Workshop returns an error

  1. Check file type (JPG, PNG, WEBP) and size (≤10 MB).
  2. Try with one or two chips first; very long stacks can hit content-policy guardrails.

Video says "upgrade needed"

AI video is a paid feature (Founding 5/day, Pro 10/day). Free and trial users see an upgrade card linking to the Founding Member waitlist.

Logo doesn't load

Hard-refresh once (or pull-to-refresh on mobile). The logo is cached aggressively at /assets/pmg-logo.png?v=6.

Run With AI returns nothing or an error

  1. Check that you haven't hit the daily fair-use limit. Free-tier limits reset every 24 hours.
  2. Try shortening the prompt — extremely long prompts can time out.
  3. If the error persists, copy the prompt and paste it into ChatGPT or Claude directly to confirm whether the prompt itself is the issue.

My vault is empty after switching browsers

The vault is local-first and lives in your browser's localStorage. To move your vault between browsers, use Export Vault on the original and Import on the new one. We're not able to recover vaults — please export periodically as a backup.
