Pricing FAQ

Quick answers to the questions we hear most often. Email support@promptmegood.com if yours isn't here.

What is the Expert Command Center?

A five-tab drawer that opens from the ⚙️ icon in the workstation topbar. Diagnose shows your prompt's strength, weaknesses, and missing context. Architect lets you toggle role, audience, format, examples, and success criteria as Off / Auto / Custom. Tune exposes behavior sliders. Variations generates Fast & Simple, Detailed & Strategic, and Bold & Persuasive versions. Save stores reusable workflows. It is a paid feature for Founding Members and Pro subscribers; everyone can use it for free during the open beta.

What does Founding Member mean?

Founding Member is a one-time $79 purchase that unlocks lifetime access to PromptMeGood's core features for as long as the service operates. Limited to the first 500 buyers. Founding Members get higher daily caps than the Free tier: 15 Run With AI, 8 image generations, 3 video generations, and 5 file analyses per day, plus the Expert Command Center. Lifetime access is at today's published caps; we may adjust caps over time to keep the service sustainable, and we'll always communicate changes by email before they take effect.

Is the price locked for life?

Yes. Founding Member is a one-time $79 charge with no recurring billing, ever. If we raise Pro prices later, your $79 never changes and you keep your founding caps for life. See the Terms for the full definition of "lifetime" (lifetime access to core features for as long as PromptMeGood operates).

What are fair use limits?

Fair use means we publish daily caps on AI-backed features (Run With AI, image generation, file analysis, video) and reserve the right to throttle or block accounts that abuse them — for example, automated/scripted use, account sharing, or sustained activity well above normal human use. Published per-day caps (Run / Image / Analysis): Free 2 / 1 / 1, Founding 15 / 8 / 5, Pro 25 / 12 / 8, Pro Studio 75 / 30 / 20 per day. The structured Prompt Builder, the Photography Suite pill pickers, and the Vault have no caps. The Photography Suite power tools (Image Workshop, Reverse Engine) and the Storyboard Generator are unlimited during the open beta but become metered paid features on July 1, 2026.The Photography Suite power tools (Image Workshop, Reverse Engine) and the Storyboard Generator are metered paid features.

Yes. Pro Monthly ($14/month), Pro Yearly ($129/year), and Pro Studio ($29/month or $290/year) launch after the Founding period. You can cancel future renewals at any time from your account — cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period, and you keep Pro access until then. There is no contract and no cancellation fee. Founding Member is a one-time purchase with nothing to cancel.

Pro Monthly, Pro Yearly, and Pro Studio are non-refundable for the current billing period after it begins, but you can cancel future renewals any time. Refund requests: email support@promptmegood.com. See the Terms for full details.

What if PromptMeGood changes features later?

We will keep core features — the Prompt Builder, Photography Suite, Video panel, Run With AI, image generation, file analysis, Vault, and history — available to Founding Members for the lifetime of the service. We may add, replace, or retire individual sub-features over time as AI providers and tools evolve, and we don't guarantee that every future Pro feature will be included in Founding. We'll communicate material changes by email, and the Founding Member price never goes up. If the service is discontinued for reasons outside our control, refunds are not guaranteed — see the Terms.

Can I use PromptMeGood for free?

Yes — the Free tier has no signup, no credit card, and no expiration. You get all three panels (Text, Photography, Video), the Prompt Builder, the Vault, and starter templates with no caps. AI-backed features are metered with daily caps: 2 Run With AI (powered by GPT-4.1-mini on Free), 1 image generation, and 1 file analysis per day. Video generation requires a paid plan (Founding Member or Pro) — the free Video panel still lets you build and copy structured video prompts to use in Sora, Runway, or any other tool. Paid plans (Founding / Pro / Pro Studio) run Run With AI on the full GPT-4.1 model. During the open beta (through July 1, 2026), the Expert Command Center, Growth Mode drawer, Image Workshop, Reverse Engine, Prompt DNA Card, and Storyboard Generator are also free for everyone — after that they become paid features on the Founding Member, Pro, and Pro Studio plans (with daily caps for the metered ones — Reverse Engine 1/5/8, Storyboard 1/5/5). The Expert Command Center, Growth Mode drawer, Image Workshop, Reverse Engine, Prompt DNA Card, and Storyboard Generator are paid features on the Founding Member, Pro, and Pro Studio plans (with daily caps for the metered ones — Reverse Engine 1/5/8, Storyboard 1/5/5).

The open beta ends on July 1, 2026. The only change is that the Expert Command Center (Diagnose, Architect, Tune, Variations, Save) becomes a paid feature available on the Founding Member ($79 one-time, lifetime), Pro ($14/mo or $129/yr), and Pro Studio ($29/mo or $290/yr) plans. What counts toward my daily usage limits?

Four AI-backed features count toward daily caps:

  • Run With AI (Free uses GPT-4.1-mini, paid uses full GPT-4.1) — Free 2 · Founding 15 · Pro 25 · Pro Studio 75 per day
  • Image generation (gpt-image-1, from the Photography Suite, Image Workshop, and DNA Card) — Free 1 · Founding 8 · Pro 12 · Pro Studio 30 per day
  • File analysis (Reverse Engine on uploaded images) — Free 1 · Founding 5 · Pro 8 · Pro Studio 20 per day
  • Video generation (Sora-style clips from the Video panel) — Free 0 · Founding 3 · Pro 5 · Pro Studio 10 per day

Everything else is unlimited: building and editing prompts in any panel, the structured Prompt Builder, all Photography Suite pills and presets, copy/tweak in the Video panel, the Vault (save / tag / export), templates, history, and "Send to ChatGPT / Claude / Gemini / Perplexity" (those use your own account on the destination, not ours). Limits reset daily at midnight UTC. Note: the Storyboard Generator and Brand Voice / Growth Mode drawer are unlimited during the open beta but become metered paid features on July 1, 2026.

What makes PromptMeGood different from other prompt builders?

Most prompt builders focus on prewritten templates. PromptMeGood focuses on helping you improve the prompt you actually want to use.

Instead of making users scroll through a huge library of generic prompts, PromptMeGood helps them clarify their goal, add useful context, improve their instructions, and structure their desired output. It is designed to reduce vague AI responses, wasted regenerations, and the frustration of not knowing how to ask AI for what they really want.

PromptMeGood is not just a prompt generator. It is a prompt improvement tool built to help users get better AI results with less trial and error.
