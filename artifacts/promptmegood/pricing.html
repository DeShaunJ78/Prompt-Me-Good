Pricing FAQ

Quick answers to the questions we hear most often. Email support@promptmegood.com if yours isn't here.

What is the Expert Command Center?

A five-tab drawer that opens from the ⚙️ icon in the workstation topbar. Diagnose shows your prompt's strength, weaknesses, and missing context. Engineer lets you toggle role, audience, format, examples, and success criteria as Off / Auto / Custom. Tune exposes behavior sliders. Variations generates Fast & Simple, Detailed & Strategic, and Bold & Persuasive versions. Save stores reusable workflows. It is a paid feature for Founding Members and Pro subscribers; everyone can use it for free during the open beta.

What does Founding Member mean?

Founding Member is a one-time $79 purchase that unlocks lifetime access to PromptMeGood's core features for as long as the service operates. Limited to the first 500 buyers. Founding Members get higher daily caps than the Free tier: 30 Run With AI, 15 image generations, and 10 file analyses per day, plus the Expert Command Center.

Is the price locked for life?

Yes. Founding Member is a one-time $79 charge with no recurring billing, ever. If we raise Pro prices later, your $79 never changes and you keep your founding caps for life. See the Terms for the full definition of "lifetime" (lifetime access to core features for as long as PromptMeGood operates).

What are fair use limits?

Fair use means we publish daily caps on AI-backed features (Run With AI, image generation, file analysis) and reserve the right to throttle or block accounts that abuse them — for example, automated/scripted use, account sharing, or sustained activity well above normal human use. The published numbers are the floor: Free 3 / 1 / 1, Founding 30 / 15 / 10, Pro 60 / 30 / 20 per day. The structured Prompt Builder, Photography Suite, and Vault have no caps.

When does checkout open?

The full beta is already free to use during the founding period. The moment Stripe checkout goes live we'll email everyone on the waitlist so the first 500 buyers can lock in $79 for life.

Can I cancel Pro?

Yes. Pro Monthly ($9/month) and Pro Yearly ($79/year) launch after the Founding period. You can cancel future renewals at any time from your account — cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period, and you keep Pro access until then. There is no contract and no cancellation fee. Founding Member is a one-time purchase with nothing to cancel.

What is your refund policy?

Founding Member purchases are refundable within 14 days of purchase if you have not used more than $5 worth of AI generations in that window. Pro Monthly and Pro Yearly are non-refundable for the current billing period after it begins, but you can cancel future renewals any time. Refund requests: email support@promptmegood.com. See the Terms for full details.

What if PromptMeGood changes features later?

We will keep core features — the Prompt Builder, Photography Suite, Video panel, Run With AI, image generation, file analysis, Vault, and history — available to Founding Members for the lifetime of the service. We may add, replace, or retire individual sub-features over time as AI providers and tools evolve, and we don't guarantee that every future Pro feature will be included in Founding. We'll communicate material changes by email, and the Founding Member price never goes up. If the service is discontinued for reasons outside our control, refunds are not guaranteed — see the Terms.
