Contact us

We're a small team and we read every message. Most replies go out within 1–2 business days.

The fastest way to reach us is email. Pick the address that matches your question — anything else can go to support@promptmegood.com and we'll route it.

Tips that help us help you faster

  • For bugs: include your browser, device, and the steps you took right before the issue. A screenshot or short screen recording is gold.
  • For billing questions: include the email used at checkout and the date of purchase. We never ask for card details over email.
  • For privacy / data deletion requests: send the email tied to your account so we can verify and act on it.
