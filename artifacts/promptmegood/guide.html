Quick Start Guide

PromptMeGood turns one honest sentence into a structured AI prompt — for text, image, or video. No signup, no install. Here is a 60-second tour.

On this page The three panels Text Photography Video Topbar Expert mode Vault

The three panels

The workstation has three inline panels. Tap the tabs at the top to switch — they all share the same canvas, so your work is one thumb-tap away.

  • Text Prompts — turn an idea into a structured prompt for ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, or Gemini. Or run it in-app with GPT-4o.
  • Photography — design photo briefs for DALL·E, Midjourney, gpt-image-1, and friends. Includes the Image Workshop photo enhancer and Reverse Engine image-to-prompt.
  • Video — direct AI video prompts for Sora-class models. Includes the 5-shot Storyboard generator.

Text Prompts in 30 seconds

1
Type one honest sentence. "Write a cold email to a SaaS director about our new analytics tool." That is enough.
2
Tap "✨ Generate My Prompt." Auto-Tune fires automatically — it reads your sentence and pre-fills the tuning panel (category, skill level, tone, format, length, language, personality) before building the prompt. You can adjust any pick and re-generate.
3
Use it. You get a structured prompt with role, context, constraints, tone, format, and next steps — plus a strength score. Copy/paste into your AI of choice, or hit Run With AI to execute it in-app with GPT-4o. Tap ✨ Auto-Boost to rewrite it once more for the strongest version.

Photography in 30 seconds

Switch to the Photography tab. The left side is your builder, the right side is the result.

  • Image Workshop — drop a JPG/PNG/WEBP (≤10 MB), pick from 15 enhancement chips (Upscale, Color Pop, Cinematic Grade, Remove BG, Restore, Day↔Night, Vector Style, Anime, Oil, Watercolor, B&W Film, 35mm Grain, Studio Lighting, Clean BG, HDR), and tap Enhance. Download as PNG or save as SVG.
  • Reverse Engine image → prompt — drop any reference image and PromptMeGood writes the prompt that would recreate it, then auto-fills the Photo Suite picks.
  • Photo Suite — tune Style, Camera & Lens (focal length, aperture, shutter, ISO, film stock), Lighting & Mood, Composition, Camera Angle, Color Palette, and Aspect with one-tap pills.
  • ⚡ Pro Tuning — Quick-Start presets, Pro Boosts, and Modes for instant cinematic looks.
  • Generate Image — runs gpt-image-1. After the result lands, tap 🧬 DNA Card to export a 1080×1350 share card that pairs the image with its exact prompt.

Video in 30 seconds

Switch to the Video tab. Type your video idea on the left, tune the Sora-style controls (shot type, camera movement, mood, style, duration, resolution), and hit Generate. Or tap 🎞️ Generate Storyboard to turn your concept into a 5-shot cinematic sequence with rendered frames — then send the whole shot list back to the Video panel as a multi-shot prompt.

The topbar

  • ❓ Help — opens this guide.
  • 🗄️ Vault — slide-in drawer with everything you have saved on this device.
  • ⚙️ Settings — opens the Expert Command Center (Diagnose · Engineer · Tune · Variations · Save).
  • Upgrade — Founding Member ($79 one-time, first 500, lifetime) or Pro ($9/mo).

Expert mode

Tap the ⚙️ gear in the topbar (any time, on any panel) to open the Expert Command Center — a five-tab side drawer for power users. Diagnose tells you what your prompt is missing and gives it a strength score. Engineer lets you toggle role, audience, format, examples, and success criteria as Off / Auto / Custom. Tune gives you behavior sliders. Variations generates Fast & Simple, Detailed & Strategic, and Bold & Persuasive versions side by side. Save stores reusable workflows. Close it any time with Esc or the × in its header — your main builder is untouched.

Free during the open beta (through June 1, 2026). After that it becomes a paid feature for Founding Member ($79 one-time, lifetime, first 500) and Pro ($9/mo) plans. Free-tier users will see a paywall card instead of the drawer.

Your Prompt Vault

Tap the 🗄️ file-cabinet icon in the topbar to slide in the Vault drawer. Everything you save — text prompts, image prompts, video prompts, DNA cards — lives here on your device, in your browser. No account required for the free tier. You can search, tag, favorite, compare two prompts side by side, export and import the whole vault as JSON, or wipe it any time. Close the drawer with Esc, the × in the header, or by tapping the dimmed area outside it. If you sign in for a paid plan, you can also opt in to cloud-backed storage so the Vault follows you across devices.
