Quick start · 60 seconds

Better prompts for ChatGPT, Claude, DALL·E, Midjourney & AI video — in one tab.

PromptMeGood is the AI prompt workstation. Type one honest sentence, get a structured, model-ready prompt back. Three panels — Text, Photography, Video — share one calm canvas. Free, no signup, local-first. This page is the speed-run; the full Manual covers every button.

The 60-second flow

Five small steps. Anything you skip, the app fills in for you.

1

Pick a panel

Three tabs at the top: Text Prompts, Photography, Video. Each is a self-contained workspace. Switch any time — your work stays put.

2

Type your goal

One honest sentence in the goal box. The strength badge turns green when you've added enough context — but you can build at any strength.

3

Tune (optional)

Tap pills to dial in tone, format, camera angle, lighting, shot size, mood. Or hit a Pro Tuning preset (Editorial Hero, Cinematic Trailer, TikTok Hook…) for an instant pro bundle.

4

Generate

One button — Generate Prompt, Build Image Prompt, or Build Video Prompt. A structured, model-ready prompt appears in the output panel in seconds.

5

Run, copy, save

Hit ▶ Run With AI for an instant answer (GPT-4o), Generate Image (DALL·E 3 / gpt-image-1), or Generate Video (AI video). Then Copy, Save To Vault, or 🧬 DNA Card for a shareable image.

★

On mobile? Pull to refresh

Swipe down from the top of the workstation to refresh — the same gesture you already know from native apps.

Three panels, one workstation

Tabs at the top swap which panel is active. Your text, picks, and history persist across all three.

✍️

Text Prompts

Plain English in, structured prompt out for ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, Gemini — anything that takes text. Run it in-app with GPT-4o or copy and paste anywhere.

  • Tone, length, format, language, and reading-level controls
  • Money Mode presets and Pro Boosts (chain-of-thought, role assign, examples, edge cases)
  • Upload a PDF or image and ask the AI about it
📸

Photography

Six pill groups — Style, Camera & Lens, Camera Angle, Lighting & Mood, Composition, Color Palette — plus the Image Workshop AI photo enhancer and Reverse Engine image-to-prompt. DALL·E 3 / gpt-image-1 powered.

  • Pro knobs: focal length, aperture, shutter, ISO, film stock
  • Quick Pick presets and Pro Tuning bundles (Editorial Hero, Cinematic Portrait, Scroll-Stopper)
  • Save as PNG, save as SVG, regenerate, or build a Prompt DNA Card
🎬

Video

Build AI video prompts with a director's tuning suite — Shot Size, Camera Angle, Camera Movement, Mood & Lighting, Style, Focus Depth, Movement Easing, Duration, Resolution. Generate full clips in-app (paid plans) or copy the prompt to any video model.

  • 10 camera angles (Eye Level, Hero, Dutch, Bird's-Eye…) and 10 movements
  • Pro Tuning presets: Cinematic Trailer · TikTok Hook · Product Reveal · B-Roll Atmospheric
  • Storyboard launcher: turn a concept into a 5-shot scene in one click

Image Workshop — your AI photo lab

Inside the Photography panel. Drop a JPG, PNG, or WEBP (up to 10 MB), pick enhancement chips, hit Enhance Photo. Powered by OpenAI gpt-image-1.

15 enhancement chips

Upscale · Color Pop · Cinematic Grade · Remove Background · Restore Old Photo · Day↔Night · Vector Style · Anime · Oil Painting · Watercolor · B&W Film · 35mm Grain · Studio Lighting · Clean BG · HDR. Stack as many as you want.

Drag, drop, done

Drag a photo straight onto the dropzone, or click to pick. Add an optional free-form note ("make the sky more dramatic") and the model honors it.

Save as PNG or SVG

One-click PNG download, plus a Save as SVG option that wraps the result in a vector container — pair it with the Vector Style chip for flat, logo-friendly output.

Three signature features

Each lives one tap away from the panel it belongs to. Free, no signup.

📸 Reverse Engine — image-to-prompt

Upload any reference image. PromptMeGood reads it with GPT-4o vision and returns a usable AI image prompt plus matching Photography Suite picks (style, camera, lighting, composition, palette). Then tweak and regenerate.

🧬 Prompt DNA Card

After you generate an image, the DNA Card composes a shareable 1080×1350 PNG that pairs the image with the exact prompt that made it. Perfect for X, Instagram, Pinterest. Built entirely on your device.

🎞️ Storyboard generator

Type a story concept; get a 5-shot cinematic storyboard with rendered images for the first two panels. Send To Video hands the shot list to the Video panel as a multi-shot prompt — instant scene plan.

Pro Tuning — pre-engineered bundles

Each panel has a Pro Tuning section with Quick Start presets, Pro Boosts, and Modes. One click loads a fully-engineered prompt scaffold — same trick the pros use, no PhD required.

Photo presets

Editorial Hero · Hero Product Shot · Cinematic Portrait · Scroll-Stopper. Plus modes: Photoreal · Magazine Polish.

Video presets

Cinematic Trailer · TikTok Hook · Product Reveal · B-Roll Atmospheric. Plus modes: Photoreal · Filmic.

Text Money Mode

Stack chain-of-thought, role assignment, examples-first, edge-case sweeps, output schema, and refusal guardrails into a single super-prompt.
