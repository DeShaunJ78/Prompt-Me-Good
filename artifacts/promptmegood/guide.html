Quick start

Your first great prompt in 60 seconds.

PromptMeGood is a calm, focused workstation for building, refining, and running AI prompts — for ChatGPT, Claude, DALL·E, and Midjourney. No signup, no copy-paste tabs, no hunting for templates. This page is the short tour. The full Manual is one click away when you want every button explained.

The 60-second flow

Five small steps. Anything you skip, the app fills in for you.

1

Type your goal

One honest sentence in What do you want AI to help you with? The strength pill above the box turns green when you've added enough detail — but you can generate at any strength.

2

(Optional) Tap Help Me Start

Not sure how to phrase it? The green Help Me Start button asks four short questions — who it's for, the outcome that matters, any rules — then fills the builder for you.

3

Generate Prompt

The big primary button at the bottom of the builder. Your structured prompt — with role, context, constraints, tone, format, and next steps baked in — appears in the output panel within a couple of seconds.

4

Refine in place

Tap More Detailed, More Bold & Direct, or Beginner Friendly to upgrade the prompt without leaving the page. Undo Last Change steps back any time.

5

Run, copy, or save

Hit ▶ Run With AI for an instant answer, Copy Prompt to take it elsewhere, or Save To Vault so you can find it later. ⌘+K opens the command palette from anywhere.

★

Returning? Welcome Back

Open the app a second time and you'll see Continue Last Prompt, Start A New Prompt, and Open Workspace right at the top — no need to scroll.

Three modes, one workstation

Switch any time using the pills at the top of the builder. Your text and settings are kept across modes, so experiment freely.

✍️

Write Something

The default. Plain English in, polished prompt out for emails, scripts, plans, study guides, content ideas — anything text.

  • Tone, length, format, and language controls
  • One-click Run With AI (GPT-4o)
  • Upload a PDF or image and ask the AI about it
🎨

Create An Image

Tap pills across Style, Camera & Lens, Lighting, Composition, and Color — then send to DALL·E 3 with one click.

  • Pro knobs: aperture, shutter, ISO, film stock
  • Surprise Me rolls a random pick
  • Download, regenerate, or use as a reference
🗂

Vault & Templates

Every saved prompt lives in the left rail. 28+ starter templates, tagging, search, compare, and full export.

  • Local-first — nothing leaves your browser
  • Tag, filter, and pin your favorites
  • Compare two prompts side-by-side

Power features worth knowing

Each one is one or two clicks from the workstation. The Manual covers them in detail.

Help Me Start

Four-question guided setup. Fastest way to get a working prompt without thinking about structure.

Run With AI

Runs your optimized prompt against GPT-4o right inside the app. Answer streams into the response panel.

Photography Suite

Five collapsible groups of pills. Tap to compose; Send To Image Generator renders with DALL·E 3.

Expert Command Center

A drawer with Diagnose, Engineer, Tune, Variations, and Save tabs. Free during open beta; paid feature after.

Command Palette (⌘K)

Search every command, mode, template, and vault item. Works from any screen.

Brand Voice Profiles

Pro. Save reusable voices and apply them with one click before you generate.

Voice Input

Dictate your goal with the microphone button. Multiple input languages supported.

File Analyze

Upload a PDF, JPG, or PNG (up to 10 MB) and have the AI read it before answering.

Vault Search & Compare

Find any saved prompt by tag, keyword, or date. Open two side-by-side to see what changed.
